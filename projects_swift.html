SWIFT

Swift Air was founded in 1997. A charter carrier servicing college athletic teams, professional sports teams, fortune 500 companies, and many more. With a fleet of Boeing 737 aircrafts it can convert its aircrafts to any configuration the client wishes and needs. Swift Air has been recognized for its high safety and operations standards. Swift focuses on training, safety, and service to provide the best service possible.

With over 20 years in the travel industry Swift Air started looking to reposition itself in the travel industry by looking at options to rebrand itself. Rebranding brings a more friendly and welcoming look with its vibrant colors yet still maintaining the Swift logo. By rebranding, Swift will still maintain its high-level service with a new image. The new web design offers a user friendly interface for current and future customers.
