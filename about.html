MEET GREGOR

Let us introduce ourselves,
We are Gregor and we are making things easier
for you, your clients and audience!

We are a small company, made up of an enthusiastic team of young, but experienced professionals from Belgrade, Serbia. We are focused on web design, web development, and digital marketing while creating sleek, recognizable, and polished brand identities for various marketing materials.

We tend to create an innovative, but still user friendly overall appearance of our client while keeping in mind the consistency of brand and specific users’ needs. At the end, the most important thing is “how the user sees your product” and adjusting the technologies to that image.

Currently, we are mostly focused on the airline industry, where we gained a great deal of our latest experience and knowledge.

We have created web solutions for several airline companies, including booking engines, user profiles for passengers, and travel agents and admin pages for the employees and agents who need specific allowances and controls. This also included the implementation of global distribution systems of our airline clients.

Also, we have been closely included in marketing strategies such as creating annual marketing plans, short-term plans, social network campaigns (Facebook, Twitter, and Google ads), press releases, and printed materials.
